Privacy Policy

Effective date: March 29, 2026

FocusFlow is a mobile app designed to help users manage focus sessions and productivity. This Privacy Policy explains what data may be collected and how it is used.

1. Data We May Collect

  • Usage Data: App interaction and performance-related data.
  • Identifiers: Device identifiers (such as IDFA, when permission is granted) for advertising and measurement.
  • Purchase Data: App Store transaction information used to manage premium purchases/subscriptions.
  • Diagnostics: Crash and technical diagnostics data used to improve app stability.

2. How We Use Data

  • Provide and improve app functionality.
  • Verify and manage premium access.
  • Display ads in the free version.
  • Monitor app performance, fix bugs, and maintain security.

3. Advertising and Tracking

FocusFlow may use third-party advertising services such as Google AdMob. Where required, the app requests permission through Apple's App Tracking Transparency (ATT) framework. If tracking permission is denied, ad personalization may be limited.

4. Third-Party Services

FocusFlow may rely on:

  • Apple services (StoreKit, iCloud/CloudKit, notifications)
  • Google AdMob (ad delivery and measurement)

These providers may process data according to their own privacy policies.

5. Data Storage

Session and settings data are primarily stored on-device. If enabled, data may sync through Apple infrastructure (such as CloudKit). Data is retained only as needed to provide app features.

6. Children's Privacy

FocusFlow is intended for a general audience and is not designed to knowingly collect personal data from children.

7. Your Choices

  • You can manage notification permissions in device settings.
  • You can manage tracking preferences in Apple privacy settings.
  • You can remove app data by deleting the app from your device.

8. Changes to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. The latest version will be made available with an updated effective date.
